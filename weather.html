Weather Plots Site

Weather Plots Site

We've been told that it is hotter near the equator, and now we've got the charts to prove it. Also, we explore whether some other weather is dependent on latitude too.

The data for these charts were generated from 500 randomly sampled lat and long coordinates. These coordinates then quired the OpenWeatherAPI and pulled measurments for one day in December 2017.

The results were then plotted using matplotlib to visualize the results. The site also contains plots for Latitude vs Cloudiness, Latitude vs Windspeed, and Latitude vs Humidity.
